# PDF

This folder contains the seven selected test PDFs used in the thesis evaluation batch. The original local project contains a larger historical PDF archive, but only the configured evaluation samples are included here to support reproducibility.

## Source and licence

The files are selected page-range extracts from the archaeological news sections of *Bulletin KNOB*, published by the Koninklijke Nederlandse Oudheidkundige Bond (KNOB). The source collection is available through the [Bulletin KNOB website](https://bulletin.knob.nl/).

Bulletin KNOB states that its publications are made available under the [Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International licence (CC BY 4.0)](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/). The journal's open-access and licensing information is described on its [About the Journal](https://bulletin.knob.nl/index.php/knob/en/about/index) page. Where an individual issue or item carries a more specific rights statement, that source-specific statement takes precedence.

The PDFs are reproduced here solely as the fixed evaluation inputs used by RefAI. Their filenames were normalised for reliable package-relative processing; the PDF contents were not editorially altered. Reuse should credit KNOB, *Bulletin KNOB*, the publication year and issue where available, the cited page range, and the CC BY 4.0 licence.
